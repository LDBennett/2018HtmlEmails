How does a ReDo Impact AG? (In our Team’s Words):
Internal ReDos

“A redo impacts all departments across the board in one way or another.”

“Disrupts production, financial cost, morale suffers.”

“This wastes the time of all team members related to the project, and it costs us money.”

“It affects the schedule of jobs, because now we have to fit a job that was already completed back into the schedule.”

 
External ReDos

“It threatens the relationship that we have with the client and may tarnish our reputation beyond that if the client speaks negatively of AlphaGraphics to others.”

“Every time we make a mistake, we also have an opportunity to make it right for the customer when handled in the correct way and win them back.”

“Easy answer: it affects profitability and client trust.”
